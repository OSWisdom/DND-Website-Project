House Rules

Soul Saves: This is a D20 test where the DM asks for a roll with no modifiers

Heroic Inspiration: When you critically miss with a 1, you are granted a point of inspirtation

Inspiration: You may roll the second advantage die after seeing the results of the first.
You do not need to call the advantage before seeing the results.

Bardic Inspiration: is a reaction that triggers when an ally fails a roll.

Guidance: is a reaction that triggers when an ally fails a roll.

The Help Action: In most instances requires the helper to be proficient in the skill they are assisting with.

Health PotionsA health potion can be used with a bonus action OR, if using a full action, you take the maximum
possible amount. Feeding a potion to someone else still requires a full action.

Group Initiative(Retired)Add all of the players initiatives together, and all of the monsters initiatives together.
whichever side has the highest number goes first.

Death Saves(Retired)Any successful death saving throws results in a critical success. Failures only reset on a long rest.

Character Creation(Suggestion)If possible, everyone should have a different main stat.

Character Creation(Suggestion) Twilight Clerics ability to give temp HP is kind of OP
