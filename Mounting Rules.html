Mounting Rules

  1. To Mount a creature you must be within 5 ft of it.
  2. Requires an action.
  3. Mounting or Dismounting requires HALF your movement speed - If your movement speed is 0 you cannot mount.
  4. For Large+ mounts - you select one of the positions on the back of the creature for you to occupy.
  5. Enemies are able to select either you or the mount as the target for their attacks.
  6. If your mount suffers forced movement - must succeed a DC10 dex saving throw - if you fail, you fall prone within 5ft of the mount
  7. The mount is either Controlled or Intelligent/Untrained.
    (As a general rule - Intelligent/Untrained creatures will not be controlled by the rider)
  8. Intelligent/Untrained mounts have their own initiative
    (Might be spooked by combat/fire, attempt to flee, attack allies)
  9. and go where they wish(Might not be where the player wants them to go)
  10. Controlled mounts may be told to Dash, Disengage, or Dodge - and act on the turn of the player who mounted it.
  11. Intelligent/Untrained may take any action on their turn.
Note: The spell Find steed allows the player to control the mount
