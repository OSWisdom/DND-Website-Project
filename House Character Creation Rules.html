House Character Creation Rules



Suggestions:

  1. It is recommended that everyone play a class with a different main stat.
  2. Please build a character that wants to go on adventures.
  3. You can play an evil character, just dont act evil towards your fellow party members

    4. and if you must, try not to undo the partys good works

  4. Try to make room in your backstory for the other characters to have been longtime associates or perhaps relatives so that the group
    feels tight-knit from the start
  5. Let the adventure we're currently running be the most exciting thing that happens to your character.


Starting Stats

Generally, players may choose from 3 diffent arrays:

15,14,13,12,10,8

15,15,13,10,10,8

15,15,15,8,8,8

If requested: 4d6 drop the lowest(x6) & Point buy are also acceptable.



Retired Rules

Flight at level 1

If you are playing a race that gains flight, you instead gain a reaction to cast the spell slowfall until level 5.
At level 5 this feature is replaced by your original flight.
